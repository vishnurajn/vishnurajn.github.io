You Reap What You Soul

3rd person puzzle platformer

You Reap What You Soul is a 3D platforming game in which the player uses their own corpse to solve puzzles. The player takes the role of a lich navigating through a macabre world inspired by the 5 stages of grief. Along this journey, the lich will be able to possess different physical bodies after the prior one has met a grisly demise.

As the player progresses they will encounter hazards such as spikes and death fog, that will encourage them to use their corpse for creative problem solving.

Mechanics: Moving, Jumping, Persistent bodies, Endless Deaths, Controlled Deaths, Hazards (Spikes etc.).

Dynamics: (How mechanics work with each other from the player’s perspective) Corpses can be used to create their own platforms by dying (either by choosing to or dying to hazards). The player is able to end their own life at any moment, so jumping and dying will result in the body being affected by gravity and momentum. This causes players to try and land their bodies into hard to reach places

  • Game Engine/Tools : Unity3d

Droppy Galaxian

Games

Droppy Galaxian is a color based matching puzzle mobile game developed in unity 3d. And published in Google Play Store

Gameplay : Defend the galaxy from this band of invading aliens known as Alien Swarm. The game is packed with an unlimited level. The player can move the bottom space ship left and right by touching the screen. And match the color of the aliens to the color of the ship by moving the ship left or right. The speed of the incoming aliens will increase over time in the game. Whenever the player kills one alien he gets one score and when the player misses one alien the game will over.
